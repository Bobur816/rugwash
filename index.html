About us

At Rugwash New York, we tailor our cleaning approach to your unique carpet needs. Our specialists start with a detailed analysis, targeting heavily soiled areas. Using advanced green cleaning methods, we ensure every corner of your carpet is thoroughly cleaned, addressing even hidden dirt.

Rugwash New York, established in 2014 in Brooklyn, New York, specializes in cleaning, repairing, and restoring Oriental/Antique rugs, wool rugs, as well as furniture restoration. Our goal is to provide top-quality professional home care services using state-of-the-art equipment and a highly skilled crew. The result is 100% satisfaction guaranteed on every job.
